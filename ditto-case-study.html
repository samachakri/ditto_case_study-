The AI Solution — Three-Stage Architecture
Three AI intervention points, each with its own trigger logic, purpose, and guardrails
P1 · Highest Priority Pre-Consultation Call — Context Collection

When it activates: Only if the "Describe your queries" field is empty, vague (<20 words), or flagged by an NLP completeness scorer below a threshold. If the user already provided sufficient detail, the AI call is skipped entirely.

What it collects: Language preference · Insurance for (self/family/parents) · Annual income range (optional) · Primary concern (protection/cost/claims). Call target: ≤ 60 seconds. All data logged to CRM before the advisor's scheduled call.

P2 · Bandwidth Recovery Post-Consultation Follow-Up

When it activates: 48–72 hours after advisor consultation, if no policy purchased and no callback scheduled. Maximum 2 AI follow-ups per user. If user shows frustration, all AI follow-ups stop permanently.

What it checks: Did user review the recommendations? Any questions remaining? Needs another advisor call? Schedules callback if needed. Does not recommend policies or quote premiums.

P3 · Claims Intake Claims Assistance — Structured Intake

When it activates: When a claim request comes in with incomplete information. AI collects policy number, insured person's name, document availability — then immediately hands off to a human claims specialist. If the user or nominee appears emotionally distressed, AI escalates instantly without completing the intake. Zero approval promises made.

Regulatory Compliance — Non-Negotiable
Two regulatory frameworks that govern every AI call Ditto makes. These cannot be designed around.
📞 TRAI · TCCCPR 2025
Telecom Commercial Communications

The TCCCPR Second Amendment (February 2025) governs all commercial calls from Indian entities.

What it requires for Ditto's AI caller:

  • All outbound telemarketing calls must use 140-series numbers. Service/transactional calls use 160-series (DoT, 2024).
  • Registration on the DLT (Distributed Ledger Technology) platform before any calls are placed.
  • Pre-consultation calls = service calls → 160-series. Follow-up calls = commercial → 140-series + DND check.
  • Explicit consent must be re-acquired within 7 days for each promotional interaction.
  • Auto-dialers and robocalls must be notified to origin access providers in advance.
  • DND registry has ~300 million registered numbers (as of 2024). All numbers must be checked before calling.

Source: Bar & Bench, March 2026 · SS Rana & Co., March 2026 · MEF, July 2025

🔒 DPDP Act 2023 + Rules 2025
Digital Personal Data Protection

The DPDP Rules were officially notified on November 14, 2025. Core compliance mandatory by May 2027 for BFSI entities (including insurance).

What it means for Nandhini's data collection:

  • Income range and family composition = personal data under the Act. Requires explicit consent before collection.
  • Language preference and insurance intent = inferred behavioral data. Consent must be tracked in a Consent Register.
  • The booking form itself can serve as the consent collection point — adding a checkbox: "I agree that Ditto may call to understand my insurance requirements better."
  • AI call recordings must be stored securely, with defined data retention limits.
  • Users have the right to withdraw consent and delete their data at any time.

Source: MeitY DPDP Rules, Nov 2025 · EY India, Dec 2025 · Grant Thornton, 2026

Ditto's advantage: Because Ditto already operates as a spam-free, consent-based advisory platform, TRAI and DPDP compliance is brand-aligned — not burdensome. Competitors who rely on aggressive outbound calling will face far greater compliance friction from these regulations than Ditto's AI system, which activates only when the user has already booked and opted into contact.

KPIs — Measuring What Actually Matters
Five KPIs selected to cover efficiency, trust, quality, conversion, and system safety
KPI 01 — Core Value Proof
Advisor Onboarding Time Reduction
Average minutes advisors spend on repetitive introductory questions per consultation, vs. pre-AI baseline. If this doesn't move, the system isn't working. Measured via advisor-reported CRM timestamps.
Target: −25% in 60 days
KPI 02 — Call Quality
AI Call Completion Rate
% of triggered calls where user stays on and provides at least 2 of 3 data points (language, coverage target, income range). Low completion = script or timing needs rework.
Target: 60% completion
KPI 03 — Brand Protection ★ Critical
Spam / Intrusion Perception Score
Post-consultation survey: "Did you find any AI interaction helpful, neutral, or intrusive?" Scored 1–5. This is Ditto's most critical brand metric. A score below 3.5 = immediate halt.
Target: 4.0 avg · Halt if <3.5
KPI 04 — Safety Guardrail
Human Escalation Rate
% of AI calls requiring immediate escalation (frustration, complex query, distress detected). Too high = AI is deployed incorrectly. Too low = possibly missing escalation moments.
Target: ~15% escalation rate
KPI 05 — Business Value
Follow-Up to Consultation Conversion Rate
% of users who received an AI follow-up and subsequently completed a second advisor consultation or purchased a policy within 14 days. This validates the ROI of replacing manual follow-ups with AI — and justifies scaling beyond the pilot segment. Measured against a control group of users who received human follow-ups only.
Target: 20% conversion uplift vs. control

Final Statement
The design constraint that governs everything above
Core Principle
The AI system proposed here is an operational assistant — not an advisor replacement. Its sole purpose is to make Ditto's human-led consultations more effective while fiercely protecting customer trust.

Human-First: Designed to empower advisors, never to replace them.
Permission-Based: Every AI interaction is conditional, consent-backed, and TRAI-compliant.
Quality Over Quantity: Success isn't measured by the number of AI calls made, but by how much more valuable the human consultations become as a result.

Ditto's 4.9/5 rating is the most valuable asset this system must protect. Any spike in intrusion perception scores — and the system stops.
